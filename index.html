Revenue Streams Overview:

  • NFT Minting Fees: One-time fee when property is tokenized (1-2% of property value)
  • Investment Commission: Fee on each investment transaction (2-3% of investment amount)
  • Rental Management Fees: Monthly fee for property management services (8% of gross rental income)
  • Transaction Fees: Fee on secondary market trades (1-2% per transaction)
  • Premium Listings: Additional fees for featured property listings
  • Subscription Services: Optional premium features and analytics for users
  • Multiple revenue streams create sustainable and scalable business model
  • Revenue grows as platform scales with more properties and investors
